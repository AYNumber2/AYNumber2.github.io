Gallery + Drink Ingredients!

Here, I'll give a little insight to some of the ingreadients that are used in making certain drinks! (With photos of course!)

  1. A3 Boba Milk Tea
  2. A13 Brown Sugar Fresh Milk with Boba
  3. C3 Fresh Lemon Honey Jasmine Green Tea
  4. K5 Strawberry Shortcake Smoothie
  5. H1 Original Bubble Waffle!



1. A3 Boba Milk Tea

This is the bread and butter of a boba tea shop. It is simply milk tea, boba (tapioca, pearls whatever you want to call it), ice, and a bit of brown sugar. The amount of brown sugar is what can be adjusted when they ask you if you want to adjust sugar. So with some drinks that are naturally sweet, I'd recommend you get lower sugar. For this drink I'd recommend 50%-70% sugar depending on if you would like it slightly more or slightly less sweet!




2. A13 Brown Sugar Fresh Milk with boba

I brought up this drink because I think that people SHOULDN'T order this. It could be a nice drink depending on your mood, but to be honest, I think we charge way too much for it. It is simply just milk, brown sugar drizzles on the inside of the cup (so it's pretty sweet), ice, and boba. This drink is also uncustomizeable as removing ice changes the consistency of the drink and you cannot change how much brown sugar we put in as it changes the appearance of the drink as well. :(




3. C3 Fresh Lemon Honey Jasmine Green Tea

This is a popular drink from our Fresh Lemon Series as bellevue square. It contains lemon chunks, lemon juice, honey water, ice, brown sugar, and green tea. This drink is pretty sweet so I'd recommend about 50%-70% sweetness again!




4. K5 Strawberry Shortcake Smoothie

A very popular drink from our Smoothie series! It's one of my favorite drinks so you should definitely try it! It contains puff cream (the yellow swirls), strawberries, strawberry syrup, milk, heavy cream, egg pudding, and ice! I think that it is pretty sweet so I'd recommend 30% sugar.

I added in a little suprise! Just kidding, I'm sorry to disappoint but I don't actually know the ingredients to our bubble waffles. However, I do know how to make them and they are dang delicious so if you would ever like a delicious sweet waffle, come visit Happy Lemon (come on Saturdays, I work then :3)
