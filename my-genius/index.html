Working At Happy Lemon!

By: Alan Yuen

Hi! My name is Alan Yuen. I'm a senior at Eastside Preparatory School. Recently, I've been working at a boba tea shop called Happy Lemon at Bellevue Square. Ever since I first had bubble tea for the first time, I've always wanted to try making it myself or simply just working at a boba shop. Over the summer, I got the opportunity to work at Happy Lemon and I became a barista-in-training. Now, I've already passed my bar test, and today I'm here to give you a little insider info on how we make bubble tea!

Obviously, I cannot diverge too many secrets, but I can still describe our process in general, and I'll talk about 4 drinks in specific in the gallery page!

General Steps:

  1. Add toppings (including extra toppings that customers ask for and yes you can do that!)
  2. For most drinks, add ice
  3. Add drink base (milk tea, green tea, black tea, etc.) or create drink base (eg. strawberry smoothie)
  4. Add crema or non-dairy milk if applicable
  5. Voila! Drink Complete! Go seal it and give to customers to enjoy!
