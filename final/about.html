About Us

My name is Thomas Yuen. Computer Surplus has been open since 2000. Our predecessor was Apex, which was open in Bellevue. We started as a wholesale retail store, but we soon provided databasing and recycling services. Now, we primarily do computer repairs and networking for small businesses (less than 50 people). We are your friendly local neighbors that are willing to help with your electrical device and networking needs, while charging reasonable prices. Come visit us!
